Haunted TV - Powered by Raspberry Pi & OpenCV

Haunted TV expands on the idea of a classic horror movie cliche: the mirror scare(a scene where a reflection is used to suddenly reveal a monster, ghost, etc.) It uses a Raspberry Pi, camera, and OpenCV to create a creepy camera feed on an old B&W TV which is triggered by face/eye detection.

SweetDreams is a dream themed home-made aircraft which was built for the 4th Redbull Flugtag in Istanbul. I decided to share our experience & build in detail to encourage you to build your own plane!
